WHY BECOME OUR CUSTOMER?

Our product range consists of quality computer equipment offered at affordable prices.

Our main goal and motivation is customer satisfaction with our services and products, so we provide a warranty of 12 to 36 months on all items.

If you are not satisfied after purchasing one of our refurbished (used) computers, our customers have the right to a refund within 7 days of purchase.

All refurbished computers and laptops in our offer are thoroughly tested, cleaned, and ready for immediate use.

When shopping in our store, you can also take advantage of installment payments with UniCredit and Raiffeisen shopping cards for up to 12 installments, with an additional 10% added to the purchase amount.
